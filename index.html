Responsive Design

As a web developer, I want to introduce you to the concept of responsive design, which is crucial for creating modern and user-friendly websites. Responsive design is an approach that ensures your website adapts and looks great on various devices, such as desktops, laptops, tablets, and smartphones. By employing fluid layouts, flexible images, and media queries, we can optimize the user experience, regardless of the screen size or orientation.
